About Me
I am Ankit Malhotra,a computer science graduate converted into product manager by profession and an entrepreneur from heart.
Ever since from my first year of engineering, I began doing coding. I applied some spectacular coding techniques to build small modules.
However, I fell in love with programming later when I started building software products from scratch in my college.
So, in the second year of engineering in 2014-15, when students are generally busy studying new subjects, I started working with startups to solve real-time problems.
In my whole college life, I worked with more than 5 startups as an intern, Some are - JungleWorks(Jugnoo), Dipper(merged into FarEye), Dogether. Apart from that, I built more than 15 products in my college life, like - live tracking of any vehicle and Brain-computer interfacing.
I joined SlicePay (One of the fastest growing startup of India), after my college to help them build their product. I worked on multiple streamlined features over there, mainly on virtual credit card and fraud detection in profile approval. The system was scaled up to 100x in terms of profile approval and order, via a very small team.
After SlicePay I moved to GameChange - An International startup based out in Dubai, worked on smart banking solutions there.
Now it was time to start the entrepreneurial journey - I along with my co-founder started building activity tracking solution for field workers. I was taking care of technology and product there. Things did not go well and we had to drop the idea.
However, failures didn’t stop me from experimenting and trying new things.
Since then I started my own IT services company in parallel and helped more than 50 companies to build their product, grow their businesses 10x faster. Most of the clients gave us the whole responsibility of product management and tech and I primarily take care of product management.
I started AlexKill, Voice assistance on the top of Alexa. Product is solving two major problems for hotel guests. One it works as a communication channel for guests, they can do anything through it which they usually do via phone call to reception and the second one is room automation. Product is sold out to one of the largest hotel chains of five stars hotel.
